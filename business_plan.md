## 1. Vision: The "Human Architect" Protocol
LumenForge was not built in Silicon Valley; it was born from the lived experience of a state-school pupil in Birmingham. The founder’s journey—navigating systemic limitations to eventually build a successful career as an architect in the USA—serves as the foundational blueprint for this platform. 

We view education not merely as knowledge transfer, but as human architecture. High-status, elite-level tutoring is currently overwhelmingly gated by geography and wealth. LumenForge’s mission is to democratize the pedagogical rigor of elite institutions (the "Millfield & Sovereign Ethos") and deliver it unconditionally to disadvantaged UK pupils via a Socratic AI mentor. We are not replacing teachers; we are providing every student with a tireless, 24/7 world-class co-pilot.

## 2. Social Value: The "1-for-2" Scholarship Engine
At the core of our business model is a sustainable, self-funding engine designed to meet and exceed government Social Value criteria. 

LumenForge operates a premium B2C tier ("Disruptor/Elite Mode") targeting affluent parents seeking competitive advantages for their children. The revenue from this B2C tier directly subsidizes our **Foundry Scholars** program. 

**The Metric:** For every `1` Elite Subscription purchased, LumenForge actively funds and provisions `2` Scholar Seats. 

These seats are distributed via an automated, verified portal utilizing `.sch.uk` email domains and DfE FSM (Free School Meals) / Pupil Premium eligibility criteria. This ensures our social impact is not an afterthought, but the primary driver of our growth architecture.

## 4. Technical Safeguarding & DfE 2026 Compliance

LumenForge anticipates the stringent requirements of the upcoming DfE 2026 AI guidelines. We have engineered a multi-layered safeguarding protocol that prioritizes student wellbeing and data sovereignty above all else.

*   **Zero-Training Data Policy:** LumenForge operates under a strict, immutable rule: student interaction data, prompts, and submitted homework are **never** used to train the underlying Large Language Models. All sessions are ephemeral at the inference layer.
*   **Active Safeguarding Intercepts:** The AI is equipped with a latent `<CRISIS_FLAG>` detection system mapped to UK safeguarding lexicons. 
*   **Automated DSL Escalation:** Upon detecting markers of learner distress or self-harm, the system asynchronously intercepts the standard response and triggers an immediate, high-priority email alert to the school's Designated Safeguarding Lead (DSL) via the `dsl@school.sch.uk` integration, including a full transcript of the interaction.
*   **Supervisor Command Centre:** Teachers are provided with a dedicated RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) dashboard to view real-time class efficacy heatmaps and review pending 'Unresolved' safeguarding alerts.
