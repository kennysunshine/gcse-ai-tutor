# AQA GCSE French (8652) Unit-Based Vocabulary

## KS3 Units (Foundation & Higher)
### Unit 1: Opinions and Cognates
- **amusant**: fun, funny
- **intéressant**: interesting
- **génial**: great, brilliant
- **bon**: good

### Unit 3: Where people live
- **habiter**: to live
- **la ville**: town/city
- **le village**: village
- **à la campagne**: in the countryside

## KS4 Units (2026 Reformed Specification)
### Unit 1: Identity and relationships with others
#### Foundation
- **le père**: father
- **la mère**: mother
- **célibataire**: single
- **sympa**: nice/friendly

#### Higher
- **le beau-père**: stepfather/father-in-law
