# AQA GCSE German (8662) Unit-Based Vocabulary

## KS3 Units (Foundation & Higher)
### Unit 1: Opinions and cognates
- **lustig**: funny, fun, enjoyable
- **interessant**: interesting
- **entspannend**: relaxing
- **toll**: great, amazing

### Unit 3: Where people live
- **der Flughafen**: airport
- **der Fluss**: river
- **die Brücke**: bridge
- **besuchen**: to visit

## KS4 Units (Defined Vocabulary List)
### Unit 1: Identity and relationships (Foundation)
- **aktiv**: active, energetic
- **alt**: old
- **ehrlich**: honest

### Unit 2: Media and technology (Higher)
- **überzeugen**: to convince, persuade
- **verbinden**: to connect, link, unite
