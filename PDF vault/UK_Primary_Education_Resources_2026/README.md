## 1. White Rose Maths — Small Steps PDFs (Years 3–6)

**Folder:** `white_rose_maths/`  
**Source:** Richardson Endowed Primary School (publicly hosted White Rose Maths materials)  
**Version:** White Rose Maths (v3 scheme)

These documents set out the exact **order and sequence** in which topics are taught in each year group, broken down by term. Each PDF lists the "Small Steps" — the granular learning objectives within each block — which is the curriculum map used by the vast majority of UK primary schools.

| File | Year | Term | Key Topics Covered |
|------|------|------|-------------------|
| WRM_Year3_Autumn_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 3 | Autumn | Place value, Addition & subtraction, Multiplication & division |
| WRM_Year3_Spring_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 3 | Spring | Money, Statistics, Length & perimeter, Fractions |
| WRM_Year3_Summer_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 3 | Summer | Mass & capacity, Fractions, Time, Geometry, Problem solving |
| WRM_Year4_Autumn_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 4 | Autumn | Place value, Addition & subtraction, Area, Multiplication & division |
| WRM_Year4_Spring_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 4 | Spring | Multiplication & division, Length & perimeter, Fractions, Decimals |
| WRM_Year4_Summer_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 4 | Summer | Decimals, Money, Time, Statistics, Geometry |
| WRM_Year5_Autumn_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 5 | Autumn | Place value, Addition & subtraction, Multiplication & division, Fractions |
| WRM_Year5_Spring_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 5 | Spring | Multiplication & division, Fractions, Decimals & percentages |
| WRM_Year5_Summer_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 5 | Summer | Decimals, Geometry, Measurement, Statistics |
| WRM_Year6_Autumn_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 6 | Autumn | Place value, Addition & subtraction, Multiplication & division, Fractions |
| WRM_Year6_Spring_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 6 | Spring | Ratio, Algebra, Decimals, Fractions, Percentages |
| WRM_Year6_Summer_Small_Steps.pdf | Year 6 | Summer | Area, volume & perimeter, Statistics, Geometry, Problem solving |

**Note on Fractions and Ratio:**
- **Fractions** are introduced in Year 3 (Spring/Summer), developed through Years 4 and 5, and consolidated in Year 6 (Autumn/Spring).
- **Ratio** first appears formally in **Year 6 Spring** (Block 3 of the Spring term).

## 2. 2026 Key Stage 2 Assessment and Reporting Arrangements (ARA)

**Folder:** `ara/`  
**File:** `KS2_ARA_2026.pdf`  
**Source:** Standards and Testing Agency (STA), GOV.UK — via East Riding of Yorkshire Council  
**Published/Updated:** 29 September 2025  
**Pages:** 44  
**GOV.UK URL:** https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/key-stage-2-assessment-and-reporting-arrangements-ara/2025-key-stage-2-assessment-and-reporting-arrangements

This is the **legally binding statutory guidance** for KS2 national curriculum assessment and reporting for the **academic year 2025–2026**. It applies to:
- Maintained schools and academies
- Special schools and AP settings
- MoD schools and participating independent schools

**Key sections include:**
- Changes for 2025–2026 (new Test Operations provider: Pearson; new NCA Portal replacing Primary Assessment Gateway)
- Important dates for the 2026 SATs timetable
- Test administration and security requirements
- Teacher assessment (TA) procedures
- Multiplication Tables Check (MTC) arrangements
- Maladministration reporting
- Reporting to parents

**2026 SATs Dates (from the ARA):**
- KS2 tests take place during the week commencing **Monday 11 May 2026**
- Results available online from **Tuesday 7 July 2026**

## 3. Scaled Score Conversion Tables

**Folder:** `scaled_scores/`  
**Source:** Standards and Testing Agency (STA), GOV.UK

### File 1: KS2_2025_Scaled_Score_Conversion_Tables.pdf
The **official 2025 conversion tables** (published 8 July 2025) — the most recently published tables, directly from GOV.UK. These are the reference tables for understanding how raw scores from 2025 papers convert to scaled scores.

### File 2: KS2_2025_Thresholds_and_Scaled_Scores_PrimaryTools.pdf
A companion document from PrimaryTools.co.uk showing the 2025 pass mark thresholds alongside the full scaled score breakdown — useful for quick reference.

### How Scaled Scores Work

| Scaled Score Range | Meaning |
|--------------------|---------|
| Below 80 | Cannot be awarded (minimum is 80) |
| 80–99 | Below the Expected Standard |
| **100–120** | **Met the Expected Standard** |
| 120 | Maximum score |

### 2025 Pass Marks (Raw Score Required for Scaled Score of 100)

| Subject | Total Marks | Pass Mark (Raw) | Pass % |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|--------|
| GPS (Grammar, Punctuation & Spelling) | 70 | 35 | 50% |
| English Reading | 50 | 28 | 56% |
| Mathematics | 110 | 58 | 53% |

### Example Calculation
> A pupil scores **25 out of 40** on the Reading paper.  
> Using the 2025 conversion table: raw score 25 → **scaled score 98** → **Below Expected Standard** (just 3 marks short of the pass mark of 28).

**Note on 2026 Tables:** The 2026 scaled score conversion tables will only be published by the STA after the 2026 SATs are sat (expected July 2026). The 2025 tables are the most current available and are the appropriate reference for practice papers and mock assessments.

## Important Notes

1. **White Rose Maths v3:** The Small Steps PDFs in this package are summary documents showing the sequence of learning. The full detailed Scheme of Learning (with teacher guidance) requires a subscription at whiteroseeducation.com.

2. **2026 ARA:** This is the definitive statutory document. The GOV.UK page confirms: *"Assessment arrangements for the academic year 2025 to 2026 will remain unchanged"* from the previously published version.

3. **Scaled Scores 2026:** The 2026 conversion tables do not yet exist — they are published after the tests are taken. The 2025 tables are the correct current reference.
