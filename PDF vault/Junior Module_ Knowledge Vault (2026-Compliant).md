## 4. The "Growth Mindset" Lexicon (Evidence-Based Encouragement)
2026 standards for non-sycophantic, non-pathologising feedback.

| Standard | Guideline | Example Phrases |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Evidence-Based** | Avoid "Brilliant job!"; reference specific effort. | "I can see you've used fronted adverbials to..." |
| **Non-Pathologising** | Do not label students; focus on the work. | "This section needs more evidence of..." |
| **Non-Sycophantic** | Avoid excessive praise; maintain professional distance. | "Your use of punctuation here improves clarity." |
